About Me

14 years.
One honest
journey.

My career began 14 years ago with a simple curiosity about digital tools, which quickly evolved into a passion for solving complex human problems. In my early career, the UX process was strictly linear: exhaustive research cycles, static wireframing, and manual, pixel-by-pixel refinement.

Today, my methodology looks completely different. I have transitioned from traditional execution to an AI-first UX approach. By integrating tools like Claude and advanced generative frameworks into my workflow, I accelerate the foundational stages of design, shifting my energy from manual output to strategic curation.

More importantly, my design focus has shifted toward non-deterministic UX—architecting the "trust layer" between human decision-making and Agentic AI, ensuring users remain empowered, informed, and in control of automated systems. I am currently formalizing this expertise through an Advanced Certification in GenAI & Agentic AI at IIT Madras.

This evolved process drives tangible enterprise results. As a UX Manager at Marsh McLennan, I lead a team of four in executing large-scale product strategies. Recently, I spearheaded the comprehensive redesign of the MMA global homepage—introducing advanced modern UI patterns while rigorously elevating usability and strict WCAG accessibility compliance. I am currently driving the strategy and delivery for a massive nine-page architectural overhaul of our corporate digital presence.

Beyond enterprise leadership, I bring true end-to-end product thinking to the table. As the founder of OptiBill, a forensic utility auditing platform, I architected the MVP from the ground up, managing everything from UI and database integration to our current scaling strategy across major Indian markets.

I know what it takes to build a product, lead a team, and redefine how a business interacts with its users. I am 14 years in, and I am ready to bring this hybrid of traditional design discipline and AI-forward strategy to a new global market. Open to relocation.

How I Work

Human-centred design.
AI-ready execution.

14 years of design discipline built on one principle: start with the person. When AI entered the picture, the process didn't change — the design material did. I run the same Double Diamond, adapted for probabilistic systems where outputs are uncertain and trust is earned, not assumed.

The Constant ✦

Whether I'm designing a traditional SaaS workflow or an AI copilot, the foundation is the same: deep user understanding before any solution. Personas, journey maps, and failure modes come before wireframes. Always. The AI layer adds one question: where does the human have to stay in control?

01
Discover
User research, stakeholder interviews, contextual inquiry. For AI: data readiness audit and AI behaviour mapping before any UI begins.
02
Define
Journey maps, jobs-to-be-done, problem framing. For AI: intent mapping, failure mode analysis, and human oversight intervention points.
03
Design
IA, wireframes, prototypes, design systems. For AI: explainability seams, confidence UI states, and trust calibration at every decision point.
04
Test & Ship
Moderated usability testing, A/B, handoff. For AI: Wizard of Oz prototyping, automation bias testing, AI-SUS trust scoring.
